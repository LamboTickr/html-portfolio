Sam's Movie Picks!

These are my top movies of the 2020's so far!

1 - Last Night in Soho

Edgar wright stepped away from his usual, more familiar comedy genre for this spooky adventure within the Soho district of London. Recommended by Sam.

2 - Top Gun: Maverick

A feel good aventure for all the family. Very little to go wrong in this movie. Tom cruise is fun! Recommended by Sam.

3 - Everything, Everywhere, All at once

A film that is so unpredictable and endearing that everyone should watch this just to understand how importantly unimportant everyting really is. Recommended by Sam.
